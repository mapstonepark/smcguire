    Mira Grant

    Mira Grant is the pen name of American author Seanan McGuire. Under the pseudonym Mira Grant, McGuire is known for her works in the science fiction, horror, and urban fantasy genres. She gained considerable acclaim for her "Newsflesh" series, which includes the novels "Feed," "Deadline," "Blackout," and various short stories set in the same universe. The series takes place in a post-apocalyptic world where zombies are a constant threat, and it incorporates elements of political intrigue and media manipulation.

    Mira Grant's writing often combines science fiction concepts with a focus on biological and medical themes. She has also written other notable works such as the "Parasitology" trilogy, beginning with the novel "Parasite," which explores the consequences of a medical breakthrough that goes wrong. Additionally, she has written standalone novels and short stories that have garnered recognition in the speculative fiction community.
